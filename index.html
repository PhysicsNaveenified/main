Class XII Chapters

All 14 chapters of Class XII Physics — electromagnetism, optics, and modern physics.

Part I — Chapters 1 to 8

Chapter 1

Electric Charges and Fields

Coulomb's law, electric field lines, Gauss's law and charge distributions.

→

Chapter 2

Electrostatic Potential and Capacitance

Electric potential, equipotential surfaces, capacitors and dielectrics.

→

Chapter 3

Current Electricity

Ohm's law, resistivity, Kirchhoff's laws, Wheatstone bridge and potentiometer.

→

Chapter 4

Moving Charges and Magnetism

Biot-Savart law, Ampere's law, force on a conductor and the cyclotron.

→

Chapter 5

Magnetism and Matter

Bar magnets, Earth's magnetic field, diamagnetism, paramagnetism, ferromagnetism.

→

Chapter 6

Electromagnetic Induction

Faraday's law, Lenz's law, motional EMF, self and mutual inductance.

→

Chapter 7

Alternating Current

AC circuits, phasors, resonance, LC oscillations and transformers.

→

Chapter 8

Electromagnetic Waves

Maxwell's equations, the EM spectrum and properties of electromagnetic waves.

→

Part II — Chapters 9 to 14

Chapter 9

Ray Optics and Optical Instruments

Reflection, refraction, lenses, mirrors, microscopes and telescopes.

→

Chapter 10

Wave Optics

Huygens' principle, interference, diffraction and polarisation of light.

→

Chapter 11

Dual Nature of Radiation and Matter

Photoelectric effect, de Broglie wavelength and wave-particle duality.

→

Chapter 12

Atoms

Bohr's model, spectral series of hydrogen and atomic energy levels.

→

Chapter 13

Nuclei

Nuclear binding energy, radioactivity, alpha/beta/gamma decay and fission-fusion.

→

Chapter 14

Semiconductor Electronics

p-n junction, diodes, transistors, logic gates and digital circuits.
